Meet the Artist!

Hey everyone! My name is Shane Basham, I am 23 years old, and I was born and raised in Tucson, Arizona. I love learning new languages, running, cooking, listening to music, playing the piano, and especially making art! On this art portfolio there are some of my best artworks displayed that I have created the last few years with a title and short description under the pictures.
